Data Deletion Process

At North Hill Cleaners, we take your privacy seriously. This page explains our data deletion process and allows you to submit a request for data deletion.

Types of Data We Store

  • Personal information (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Order history and transaction records
  • Customer preferences and special instructions
  • Communication history
  • Website usage data

Deletion Process

  1. Submit the data deletion request form below
  2. We will verify your identity within 2 business days
  3. Upon verification, we will begin the deletion process
  4. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete
  5. Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes

Timeline

We aim to complete all data deletion requests within 30 days of verification. You will receive regular updates on the progress of your request.
