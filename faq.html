Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What if my stain doesn't come out?

While we use advanced stain removal techniques and specialized products, some stains may be permanent or require multiple treatments. We will always inform you before proceeding with additional treatments and provide a detailed assessment of the stain's condition.

What happens if my item is damaged?

We take every precaution to prevent damage to your garments. In the rare event of damage, we have a comprehensive insurance policy and will work with you to resolve the situation fairly. Our team is trained to handle delicate items with the utmost care.

Stain Removal Policy

Our stain removal process follows these steps:

  1. Initial assessment of the stain type and fabric
  2. Selection of appropriate cleaning method and products
  3. Pre-treatment of the stain
  4. Main cleaning process
  5. Post-treatment inspection
  6. Final quality check

We use eco-friendly products and follow industry best practices to ensure the best possible results while maintaining fabric integrity.

Damage Policy

Our damage policy includes:

  • Comprehensive insurance coverage for all items
  • Detailed documentation of item condition before and after cleaning
  • Immediate notification if any issues arise during cleaning
  • Fair compensation or replacement options in case of damage
  • Transparent communication throughout the process

We maintain detailed records of all cleaning processes and are committed to resolving any issues promptly and professionally.

Our Expertise

With over 15 years of experience, our team has developed expertise in handling various types of garments and fabrics:

  • Delicate fabrics and specialty materials
  • Wedding gowns and formal wear
  • Leather and suede items
  • Antique and vintage clothing
  • Structured garments and suits
  • Designer labels and luxury items

Our staff undergoes regular training to stay updated with the latest cleaning techniques and industry standards.
